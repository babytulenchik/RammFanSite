RAMMSTEIN HISTORY

Explore the journey of Rammstein through their most important milestones and iconic music videos.

1994
January 1994

The Birth of Rammstein

Rammstein was formed in Berlin by guitarist Richard Z. Kruspe. The band's name was inspired by the Ramstein airshow disaster, adding an extra 'm' to make it "Rammstein" (ramming stone).
Formation Berlin Original Lineup
1995
September 1995

"Herzeleid" - Debut Album

Release of their first studio album "Herzeleid" (Heartache). The album established their signature industrial metal sound and laid the foundation for their future success.
First Album Industrial Metal German Lyrics
1997
August 1997

"Sehnsucht" & "Du Hast"

Release of the second album "Sehnsucht" (Longing) featuring the iconic single "Du Hast". The song became their international breakthrough and one of their most recognizable tracks.
International Breakthrough MTV Award Iconic Single
2001
April 2001

"Mutter" - Critical Acclaim

"Mutter" (Mother) is released, considered by many fans and critics as their magnum opus. The album featured a more refined sound and complex lyrical themes.
Critical Success Concept Album Gold Certification
2004
October 2004

"Reise, Reise" & "Rosenrot"

Release of "Reise, Reise" (Arise, Arise) followed by "Rosenrot" (Rose Red). These albums continued their exploration of diverse musical styles and themes.
Nautical Themes Controversial Videos European Success
2009
October 2009

"Liebe ist für alle da"

Release of their sixth studio album "Liebe ist für alle da" (Love is there for everyone). The album featured some of their most controversial lyrics and marked a return to heavier sounds.
Explicit Content Banned in Germany Heavy Sound
2019
May 2019

The Long-Awaited Return

After 10 years of silence, Rammstein returns with their self-titled seventh album. The release was accompanied by a massive stadium tour across Europe.
10-Year Hiatus Stadium Tour Chart Topper
2022
April 2022

"Zeit" - Reflections on Time

Release of their eighth studio album "Zeit" (Time), exploring themes of mortality, time, and reflection. The album shows a more mature and contemplative side of the band.
Mature Themes Reflective Lyrics Critical Acclaim
